Chocolate truffles is so good and easy to make with just two ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • Sweetened Cream
  • Chocolate Powder or Milo

How to made it:

  1. Put chocolate powder and sweetend cream onto a bowl and mix it well.
  2. Grab a generous amount of the mixture and shape it into a ball.
  3. Gather all of the ball-shaped truffles into a plate and put them into a fridge.
  4. You can add a chocolate powder on top of the truffles and enjoy them.
