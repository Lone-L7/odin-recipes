Crepes are good and simple to make. It is also customizable as well.

Ingredients:

  • One cup of Flour
  • 5 tablespoons of Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of Salt
  • Two Eggs
  • Half a cup of Water

How to make it:

  1. Put the flour as well as sugar, salt and eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add a little bit of water into the bowl and stir it well.
  3. As you continue to stir it, add little by little more of the water.
  4. After the mixture has been stirred well, warm up your pan and add either oil or butter.
  5. Grab your ladel, take abit of the batter and spread it thinly onto the pan.
  6. Let it cooked one side until there is no longer a wet batter. Flip it once it is ready.
  7. Wait once again until the crepes turned golden and abit burned.
  8. Add toppings to your crepes according to your liking and it is ready to serve!
