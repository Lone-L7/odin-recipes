French fries is an iconic easy snack to make on your own. Perfect for movie times.

Ingredients:

  • Potato
  • Salt
  1. Cut the potato into a rectangular shape.
  2. Grab a pot and add a generous amount of oil into the pot. Let them warm up for few minutes until the oil is starting to bubble up.
  3. Put the cut-up potato into the pot and stir it slowly for a bit.
  4. Let them fry until the potato forms a crispy, golden texture.
  5. Prepare a plate with tissue on it and transfer the fries into that plate.
  6. Let the oil soaked up from the fries before serving them.
